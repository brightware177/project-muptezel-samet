Our company

KM-Express is located in Antwerp, Belgium. We have networks of partners around the world to offer you comprehensive and extremely high freight services. Our company has grown rapidly thanks to the success of a solid team structure within the company. We have been successful because our company strives for very simple honesty, reliability and innovation. These three points are very important to us and we strive to achieve this for every task.

By choosing KM-Express, you have opted for one of the most qualitative express services in Belgium that is committed for the safe and timely movement of freight. Our capacities in the field of international transport are supported by specialized personnel with extensive experience in customs procedures. We regard our customers as partners. And we always strive for a long-term, solid relationship and continue to look for improvement and optimization.
