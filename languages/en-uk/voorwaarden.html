Terms & conditions


Price calculation

Kilometers are counted back and forth from Wilrijk starting point and are calculated using TLN planner. A discount of 5% is granted for journeys over 500 km. This has always been taken into account in price requests. Extra costs such as toll, customs, ferry, etc. are charged to the customer. You can always request an all-in price for a specific ride. Waiting times: first 30 minutes free (if exceeded they will be charged)


Follow-up

All our cars are accessible with GSM. For Track & Trace we work together with Nigual.


Refund

Refunds are always brought in, checked at our office and delivered at the next order. We do not charge extra costs for this.


Billing

Happens every 15 days or weekly if desired.


Terms of payment

Payments are always made at the latest 30 days after the invoice date.


Insurance

All our transports are subject to the general CMR conditions. If desired, we can insure certain transports extra.
