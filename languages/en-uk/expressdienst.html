Express service

You can count on us for national as well as international (Europe) express drives. From call we send as soon as possible or by appointment a suitable car for collection. This exclusively goes to the desired delivery address. Multiple stops are possible. Kilometers are always calculated back and forth from the departure point of Antwerp. We give a 5% discount for journeys above 500 km. This applies to all vehicle types. Extra costs such as toll, customs, ferry, etc. are always charged. Give us a call and we calculate an all-in price for a specific transport.
